<What this protocol protects or enables.>

- <scope>

- <non-scope>

| Item | Location |
|---|---|
| Canonical rule | <path> |
| Consumers | <path or description> |
| Validation | <command, script, tool, or manual check> |

## Procedure

3. <step>

- <condition>

- <validation interface>

- [ ] <check>
